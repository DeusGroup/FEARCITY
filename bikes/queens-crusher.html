Old School Authority

The Queens Crusher doesn't need to prove anything to anyone. It carries the weight of tradition and the respect that comes from staying true to original outlaw values. This bobber represents a time when motorcycles were built by rebels, for rebels.

Every line pays homage to the original bike builders who refused to conform. When you ride the Crusher, you're carrying forward a legacy that predates corporate influence and mass production.
